AirSync Privacy Policy

At AirSync, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how it is used, and your rights. Effective date: August 8, 2025

Information We Collect

AirSync collects and processes the following data locally on your device:

  • Notifications: Accessed via the Notification Listener to mirror your notifications to your connected macOS device.
  • Clipboard Data: If enabled, your clipboard contents may be synced across your devices. (If the app is in focus)
  • Media Metadata: Such as currently playing song/artist, used for displaying playback controls on macOS.
  • Network Information: Your local IP address is used to communicate securely between your Android and Mac devices over Wi-Fi.
  • Wallpaper (Optional): Used to customize the UI on macOS with your Android wallpaper. This does not involve uploading or sharing your files.
  • App List (Optional): Used to identify the app sending the notification, shown as part of the mirrored notification.

How We Use Your Data

All data processed by AirSync is:

  • Stored locally on your devices.
  • Never uploaded to any server or cloud service.
  • Only transmitted via an encrypted local WebSocket connection (WSS) between your Android and macOS devices.

AirSync does not:

  • Collect analytics or crash data
  • Serve ads or use tracking technologies
  • Share data with any third-party services

Permissions Explanation

Permission Purpose
INTERNET, ACCESS_WIFI_STATE For local device communication and app updates
BIND_NOTIFICATION_LISTENER_SERVICE To mirror notifications to your Mac
FOREGROUND_SERVICE Required for background syncing and to prevent app being killed
REQUEST_IGNORE_BATTERY_OPTIMIZATIONS Ensures AirSync stays connected
READ_CLIPBOARD Optional clipboard sync if app is in focus
READ_WALLPAPER_INTERNAL or WallpaperManager To show your wallpaper on macOS
POST_NOTIFICATIONS To display relevant local notifications on connection status

Security

AirSync uses TLS (WSS) AES 256-bit encryption to secure all communication between devices. A shared encryption key is exchanged locally during the pairing process and is never transmitted over the internet.

Data Retention

No personal data is stored permanently. All mirrored notifications and clipboard data are stored only in RAM or app sandbox storage and are cleared automatically after use.

Your Rights

Since AirSync does not collect or store any personal data on external servers, there's no user data to delete or export. All usage is local and under your full control.
