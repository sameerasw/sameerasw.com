## 1.3 [Deprecated] Android App (Side-loading)

1. Download the latest app-release.apk from GitHub releases.
    
    https://github.com/sameerasw/airsync-android/releases/latest
    
2. Install the downloaded apk file. 
    
    <aside>
    ⚠️
    
    Installation might get blocked by play protect due to the app using sensitive permissions and being sideloaded. If there is no visible option to skip the check, you may need to go to Google Play Store > Account menu > Play Protect and disable temporarily during the installation.
    
    ![Play Protect](attachment:9fc95473-bb14-4ca2-9c81-39d876402ac9:CleanShot_2025-08-19_at_10_.32.182x.png)
    
    </aside>
    
    <aside>
    ⚠️
    
    OEM specific sideloading prevention features like Advanced Protection Mode in Pixels, Auto Blocker in Samsung OneUI and more, You may have to go into those settings and temporarily disable them during the installation.
    
    ![Auto Blocker 1](attachment:13e168eb-6e42-4956-a3d1-4f11b3e9ad79:CleanShot_2025-08-19_at_10_.34.542x.png)
    
    ![Auto Blocker 2](attachment:001392c1-9d10-4338-8389-a8d9c2481bb7:image.png)
    
    </aside>
